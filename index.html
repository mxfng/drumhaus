Drumhaus — Online Drum Machine

A drum machine for the browser.

Drumhaus is an online drum machine and beat sequencer. Build beats across 8 voices and 16 steps with four chainable pattern variations up to 8 bars. Per-step velocity, flam, ratchet, and accent. Per-voice filters, pitch, decay, pan, and mute/solo. Master FX bus with reverb, compressor, phaser, and saturation. Export to WAV or share presets via link. Free, no account required.

Built by Max Fung. Source on GitHub.
