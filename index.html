Drumhaus — Online Drum Machine and Beat Sequencer

A drum machine for the browser.

Drumhaus is an online drum machine and beat sequencer with deep sound shaping. Shape every voice with filters, pitch, decay, and pan. Build beats across 8 voices with chainable patterns, velocity, flam, ratchet, and accent. Export to WAV, share presets via link, or save locally. No download, no account.

Sequencer

Program beats across 8 voices and 16 steps with four chainable A/B/C/D pattern variations, combinable up to 8 bars. Per-step velocity, flam, ratchet, and accent controls add micro-rhythm and expression. Per-voice timing nudges push hits off the grid for a more human feel.

Sound

Each voice has a decay envelope, split high-pass and low-pass filters, panning, volume, semitone pitch adjustment, and mute/solo. The master bus adds saturation, phaser, reverb, and a compressor. Rotary controls respond on logarithmic curves tuned to how we actually hear.

Presets and Export

Save presets to local memory, export them as .dh files, or share entire rig snapshots as compressed URLs. Export patterns to WAV via offline rendering with configurable bar length and FX tail.

More

  • Curated sample kits with instant hot-swapping and waveform visualization
  • Click-drag step entry, copy/paste/clear, keyboard shortcuts
  • Night mode with animated sky
  • Responsive layout scaling for different screen sizes
  • Installable offline-capable PWA

No server, no database, no account. Drumhaus is fully static and runs anywhere.
